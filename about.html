Looking down a telescope taking a picture of my reflection in the objective mirror. If you look closely, you can see repeating, smaller reflections in the focus mirror. I like my somewhat surprised expression, and the slightly goofy lesson the picture presents: looking down a tool intended to look at distant stars, I see only myself.
