I am an Earth and Planetary Sciences master’s student at Harvard University. My research has focused on trace atmospheric gases. I have enjoyed learning technical skills including how to use the Harvard Odyssey Computing Cluster, as well as how to interact with data structures I had not seen outside of EPS research. This experiential learning component is key to learning about the world and why it’s so awesome to study earth science.
